Services

With over 38 years experience in the Tree Surgery Industry, ACT Tree Felling can provide all your tree removal, pruning and chipping requirements.

 

Tree felling

We always prefer to retain trees where possible, however when removal is necessary you can be assured that your trees will be brought down safely and efficiently, with the area left spotlessly clean after the job is complete. Our arborists can also advise you on the life expectancy of your trees, so you can plan ahead, if need be.

Your tree may need removing if it is:

  • Dead or diseased;
  • Storm damaged;
  • In a dangerous or unstable condition; or
  • Planted in an inappropriate position given itsâ€™ current or potential growth size.
 

Pruning & shaping

ACT Tree Felling are experts when it comes to pruning trees and it is a service we really pride ourselves on. There are many benefits to pruning a tree correctly and we always prune to the highest standard, with an understanding of tree biology and the variance of species characteristics.

Correct pruning can reduce the wind resistance of your trees, lessening the chance of branch failure.

Your trees may require pruning if they have:

  • Excessive end weight (the amount of foliage concentrated towards the end of branches);
  • Structural defects such as inclusions, cavities, rot or diseases;
  • Lower branches that require clearing to allow for pedestrian or mower access.

Or pruning may be beneficial in order to:

  • Allow more light to penetrate to your garden or grass;
  • Remove large limbs hanging over your house;
  • Remove dead wood or diseased branches;
  • Clear from power lines, other structures or buildings.
 

Dead-wooding

Dead wooding is the process of selectively removing the dead, dying and diseased branches from trees. If dead wood is removed on a regular basis, a tree remains in good condition, puts on more growth and generally has a longer and healthier life.

This service provides numerous benefits including:

  • Increasing the safety of the area surrounding the tree;
  • Promoting positive health of the tree by stimulating new growth;
  • Reducing the amount of leaf material, as a surplus of material can encourage insect infestation and/or the spread of disease;
  • Maintaining the aesthetic appeal of the tree.

Power line clearing (ActewAGL Accredited)

All home owners in the ACT are required by law to ensure their trees are clear from power and service lines. Sometimes trees growing too close to power lines can cause blackouts, create safety hazards such as grass fires and bushfires, and increase the risk of electrocution.

Our staff are trained to work around live power lines, so most of the time you wonâ€™t need to have the power turned off to clear your trees from the lines. In cases where a power outage is required, we will arrange the whole process for you in cooperation with ActewAGL.

You can rest assured that we are:

  • ActewAGL qualified to the highest level, to work with trees around power lines;
  • Fully trained by TAFE NSW in Vegetation Management;
  • All employees are trained and re-assessed yearly to maintain these qualifications and keep up-to-date with changes and new methods of work.
 

Cable and bracing

Trees are very beneficial to our environment and add enormous value to our homes and places of work. They may also have sentimental value for some customers, who may wish to retain their trees at all costs.

However sometimes trees with multiple trunks, or what we call â€˜co-dominant stemsâ€™, can become structurally defective and â€˜failureâ€™ at this type of branch union is quite common.

ACT Tree Felling can inspect your trees, assess them for any structural weakness and then recommend the best type of cable or bracing system for your situation. The result? You can retain the trees you love and sleep well at night knowing that they, you and your property are safe.

Stump grinding and chipping

Tree stumps can be unsightly, obtrusive and hazardous. Stump grinding is the most efficient and cost-effective solution to removing unwanted stumps from your property, to prevent re-growth and the spread of tree disease.

Stumps can become:

  • Nesting sites for termites;
  • Trip hazards;
  • Unsightly in grassed areas; and
  • Full-grown trees again in future.

We also offer a wood chipping service where we can put all your branches/cuttings through our chipper, then either take away the mulched material or leave on-site for you to apply to your garden

 

Mulch Orders

Applying mulch to your trees and garden looks great, retains soil moisture, slows down the weeds and can add nutrition to your plants and trees. Mulch is also great for trees in decline, as it aids recovery from stress, injury or disease.

ACT Tree Felling can supply quality mulch at a fantastically cheap rate, to locations throughout Canberra and Queanbeyan so please give us a call and we can get you onto our popular waiting list.
