About Us

With over 38 years experience in the Tree Surgery Industry, ACT Tree Felling can provide all your tree removal, pruning and chipping requirements.

About ACT Tree Felling

A.C.T Tree Felling is Canberra’s longest running tree services business, established by Paddy Hanson in 1975.

What began as a small family-run business, between Paddy and his son Tim van der Linden, has now progressed into one of the leading Arboriculture service providers in Canberra.

We are professional Arborists who have a passion for trees and a genuine interest in maintaining their health and safety for the enhancement of our neighbourhoods and communities. Our service provides total tree health care, ranging from specialist pruning, dead-wooding, removal, stump grinding, cable and bracing, and power line clearing.

We have provided our specialist services for many established clients over the years, including; The Prime Minister’s Lodge, The Australian National Botanic Gardens, Territory and Municipal Services, Civium Strata People, Independent Strata Management, and A.C.T Strata Management. We are also the preferred tree services supplier for the majority of Canberra’s real estate agencies, including Peter Blackshaw Real Estate, Luton Properties, Elders Real Estate, and Ray White.

As an individual home owner you may only ever use a qualified tree surgeon once or twice; or you may require one on a more regular basis, for example if you are maintaining trees near power lines, or if you own a specimen tree requiring regular pruning. Whatever your situation, it is extremely important to choose a professional tree surgeon who won’t over-charge you, or worse yet, provide poor quality work to your trees which can’t be reversed. At A.C.T Tree Felling we provide professional tree advice, high quality work, fair pricing, reliability and friendliness that comes with being a progressive tree services provider.

So you can rest assured that we are the right choice for all your tree service needs.

All of our tree climbers are either Qualified Arborists (minimum Cert. III) or are currently undertaking training within our company in conjunction with accredited arboriculture training conducted at C.I.T, Canberra.

All of our employees have current certificates and training in the following: Apply First Aid, Chainsaw Operations, Tree Climbing Techniques, Asbestos Awareness and Industry White Cards.

A.C.T Tree Felling is an ActewAGL Accredited tree-trimming provider, qualified to the highest level (i.e. High Voltage).

A.C.T Tree Felling holds $20,000,000 Public Liability Insurance with QBE Insurance. (Policy No. 41A992499BPK).

A.C.T Tree Felling holds $1,000,000 Professional Indemnity Insurance with QBE Insurance (Policy No. 41A043327PID).

A.C.T Tree Felling holds Workers Compensation Insurance with CGU Workers Compensation (Policy No. O/12-16087).

A.C.T Tree Felling holds Comprehensive Vehicle Insurance with Zurich Steadfast Business Insurance (Policy No. 089149PZB1).

Copies of all our insurances and qualifications are available upon request.
